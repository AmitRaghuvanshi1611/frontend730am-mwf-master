Mukesh Ambani

Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani (born 19 April 1957) is an Indian billionaire and businessman who is the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries.[4] As of May 2025, he is the richest person in Asia and 13th richest in the world, with a net worth of US$108 billion
